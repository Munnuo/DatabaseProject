About De'Lanco's Cupcakes

Founded in August of 2016 and based in Los Angeles California De'Lano's is becoming a nationwide cupcake bakery that produces quality, appetizing, affordable cupcakes. We try our hardest to make you the customer feel like you are part of the De'Lanco family by presenting sterling customer service as well as utilizing suggestions from the customers from our surveys. This is to insure that we are up to date on the popular flavors to intice your taste buds as well as taking your inputs and implementing them into something tasty. So, in a way you the customers help us produce products just off your inputs. De'Lanco's Cupcakes started off as a small shop on Rodeo Drive only producing cupcakes. we are now trying to expand not only our locations, but as well as our products by not only producing cupcakes but cookies, cakes, pies, brownies, as well as other pasties so that we can attract a more vast clientele. We hope to satisfy your needs.
